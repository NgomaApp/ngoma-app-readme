# Ngoma

## 🌍 Overview
Ngoma is a digital platform connecting verified traditional healers and spiritual practitioners with users seeking guidance, consultations, and healing products.

We are building the trust layer for spiritual commerce in Africa.

---

## 📱 Live Application
Ngoma is currently live on:

- Apple App Store
- Google Play Store

Users can:
- Discover verified healers
- Book consultations
- Access spiritual services safely and privately

---

## 🚀 Current Focus
We are in an early-stage activation phase, focused on:

- Supporting healers to onboard successfully
- Enabling healers to bring their existing patients onto the platform
- Driving first consultations and usage
- Building trust through real transactions and payouts

---

## 📊 Traction
- 14 healers onboarded
- Growing supply pipeline
- Initial user activity in progress

---

## 💡 Problem
The spiritual and healing economy operates largely in the informal sector, resulting in:
- Lack of trust and verification
- No structured access to practitioners
- Payment and safety risks for users

---

## 🔧 Solution
Ngoma provides:
- Verified healer marketplace
- Structured booking system
- Secure payments and payouts
- Privacy-first access to services

---

## 🎯 Vision
To formalise and digitise Africa’s spiritual economy, enabling trusted access, income stability, and scalable infrastructure.
